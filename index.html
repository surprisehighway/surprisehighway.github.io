Hello World

SurpriseHighway.github.io is a selection of of development tools, code libraries, and utilities that we use everyday at Surprise Highway. Although we include technical code examples, we hope this can be a resource for our designer partners as well as our developers.

Working with us on a new project? Chances are we've already shared this with you. Feel free to send us a link to any of the examples that might suit your project. Want to make a tweak? Not a problem. We can work with anything you see here to match your visual design and functional vision.

Want to contribute? Awesome!
Hit us up at GitHub, submit a pull request, and help us grow.

Frontend

Our favorite frameworks, libraries, and widgets. Animations, UI patterns, and interactive components are listed here.
